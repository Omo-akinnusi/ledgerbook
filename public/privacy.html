1 Who We Are

Cash Counter is a business finance tracking application developed and operated by VBook Enterprise. We are registered in Nigeria and are the data controller responsible for your personal information under Nigeria's Data Protection Act 2023.

Contact us at any time:

  • Email: [email protected]
  • App: https://cashcounter.vbookng.com

2 Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Your name and email address, collected when you sign in via Google or email/password authentication.
  • Your business name, tagline, logo, and branding preferences that you configure within the App.
  • Financial records you enter into the App, including income amounts, expense amounts, categories, and transaction notes.
  • Support messages you send through the in-app chat widget, including the content of your message and the topic you selected.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device type, browser, operating system, and screen resolution — collected by Sentry (our error monitoring provider) to help us diagnose and fix technical issues.
  • Error logs, crash reports, and performance data generated during your use of the App.
  • Your approximate location (country level only), inferred from your IP address, used solely for analytics and compliance purposes. We do not collect your precise location.

3 How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate your identity when you sign in.
  • To store and sync your financial records securely across your devices in real time.
  • To generate reports, exports, and summaries that you request within the App.
  • To receive and respond to support messages you send us through the in-app chat.
  • To detect, diagnose, and fix bugs, errors, and performance issues using error monitoring tools.
  • To improve the App's features, design, and overall user experience.
  • To comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations under Nigerian law and other applicable laws.

⚡ We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, resale, or any purpose other than providing the App's core functionality.

4 Legal Basis for Processing

Under Nigeria's Data Protection Act 2023 and, where applicable, the GDPR, we process your personal data on the following lawful bases:

  • Contractual necessity: processing required to provide the App and its features to you.
  • Legitimate interests: error monitoring and performance improvement to ensure App stability.
  • Legal obligation: compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Consent: where you have actively provided information (such as support messages), your act of submission constitutes consent.

5 How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored using Google Firebase, a cloud platform operated by Google LLC. Specifically:

  • Authentication data (name and email) is managed by Firebase Authentication.
  • Financial records, settings, and branding data are stored in Google Firestore, hosted in the European Union (europe-west1 region).
  • Error and crash data is processed and stored by Sentry, Inc., whose servers are located in the European Union.

We implement Firestore security rules that ensure only you — authenticated with your own account — can read or write your data. No other user can access your records.

6 Data Retention

  • Your financial records, settings, and account data are retained until you delete your account or request deletion.
  • Upon account deletion or a valid deletion request, all records will be permanently erased within 30 days.
  • Error logs held by Sentry are retained for 90 days before automatic deletion.
  • Support messages sent via WhatsApp are subject to WhatsApp's own retention policies once delivered.

8 International Data Transfers

As a Nigerian-based service, your data may be processed outside Nigeria — specifically in the European Union (Firebase Firestore and Sentry) and the United States (Vercel and Meta). Where such transfers occur, we rely on:

  • Standard contractual clauses and data processing agreements provided by each service provider.
  • The European Union's adequacy mechanisms, which apply to Firestore and Sentry data stored within the EU.
  • Compliance with Nigeria's Data Protection Act 2023, which governs cross-border data transfers from Nigeria.

9 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: Request that we permanently delete your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Request your financial data in a structured, machine-readable format. CSV export is available directly within the App.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing, including profiling (we do not engage in profiling).
  • Restriction: Request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.

📌 To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will acknowledge your request within 72 hours and respond fully within 30 days.

10 Children's Privacy

Cash Counter is not directed at or intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account or provided us with their personal data, please contact us immediately at [email protected] and we will delete the data promptly.

11 Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or behavioural analytics tools. The following essential technologies are used solely to operate the App:

  • Firebase Authentication session tokens — required to keep you logged in securely.
  • Sentry session identifiers — required to link error reports to a browser session for debugging. These do not identify you personally.

These are strictly necessary for the App to function and cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality. No personal financial data is included in these session identifiers.

12 Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS on all connections.
  • Firebase Authentication tokens with automatic expiry and refresh.
  • Firestore security rules that restrict each user's data to their own authenticated account.
  • Continuous error and anomaly monitoring via Sentry with EU-based data storage.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We will notify affected users promptly in the event of a data breach as required by applicable law.

When we do, we will revise the Effective Date at the top of this document and, where material changes are made, notify users via a notice within the App. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Policy.
